1. What are CSS combinators

Ans:
They are of 4 types
Space combinators
Syntax:selector1 selector2
Child Sector
Syntax:selector1>selector2
adjacent sibling selector
Syntax:selector1+selector2
all adjacent selectors
Syntax:selector1~selector2

2. What are pseudo selectors in CSS

Ans:
It mainly contains 2 divisions. They are:
1. Pseudo Classes
Syntax:selector: pseudo-classname
         For example:
         Lorem ipsum dolor sit, amet consectetur adipisicing.
1. Pseudo Element
Syntax:selector:: pseudo-element
         
 For example:
         Lorem ipsum dolor sit, amet consectetur adipisicing.
